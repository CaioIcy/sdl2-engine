Detailed Description

Temporary buffer that grabs log messages from stream.

Usage examples: 1) log_debug() << "Hello world."

Will print the message "[DEBUG] - Hello world.", since the LogBuffer created will immediately be destroyed/go out of scope.

2) LogBuffer test_log = log_warn(); test_log << "One two "; test_log << "three."; 

   After test_log goes out of scope, it will print "[WARNING] One two three.".
Note
The available functions to log are 'log_debug()', 'log_info()', 'log_warn()', 'log_error()'.
